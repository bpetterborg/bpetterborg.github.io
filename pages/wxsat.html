Satellite Imagery

Pictures of Earth from space, captured by NOAA's APT satellites, downloaded with my equipment.

I listen to the downlink of the satellite with an RTL-SDR using SigDigger, which I decode with NOAA-APT. Newer images should be in color and have maps, but old ones will not.
